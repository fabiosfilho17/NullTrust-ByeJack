# Threat Model (Simple)
=============================================================

## What I'm Protecting
- Accounts and identities (mainly privileged accounts)
- Access to sensitive apps (simulated)

## Main Risks (High Level)
- Account takeover due to lack of MFA / phishing
- Unauthorized access by poorly controlled privileged account
- Legacy authentication / old protocols
- Out-of-bounds contractor access

## Controls I will demonstrate
- MFA + Conditional Access
- Restrictions by group/risk/app
- Documented exceptions (break-glass) and why
